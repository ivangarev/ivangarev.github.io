11 MAINTENANCE

CPU resetting
If the IC-R8500 is behaving erratically, this may be an
indication of a CPU malfunction. In such cases, reset
the CPU.
While pushing [MW], push [POWER] in to turn on the
receiver power.
• The CPU is reset.
NOTE: Resetting the CPU clears all memory contents
and returns internal settings to their factory
defaults
